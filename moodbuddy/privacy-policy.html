MoodBuddy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 February 2026

MoodBuddy ("the App") is developed by Nothrix Labs ("we", "us", "our"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App handles and how it is stored.

1. Data Storage — Local Only

All data you enter into MoodBuddy — including mood entries, tags, notes, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's IndexedDB storage. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal data.

Because your data never leaves your device, we cannot access, recover, or view it.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Personal information (name, email, account details)
  • Health or mood data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

3. Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device and interaction data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share any of your mood or health data with ad providers.

You can opt out of personalised ads through your device's advertising settings.

4. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

5. Data Export

The App allows you to export your data as a file. This export is generated entirely on your device. We have no access to exported files.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party SDKs or analytics services are integrated.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us via the contact form within the App.
